Strawberry

  • Hi, my name is Strawberry! I am one of the most famous fruits. I am a large and plump, cone shaped fruit that is red and shiny. I have seeds speckled all over my outer layer, and a green stem on my bottom. I am very sweet!

Blueberry

  • Hi, my name is Blueberry! Despite the name, I am really dark purple. I have a thin purple skin that pops when I am eaten. I have a juicy soft inside, with small seeds. I can be tart, or sweet!

Watermelon

  • Hi, my name is watermelon. I am a big red fruit, with a sweet and juicy taste. I have a hard, bitter outer rind, with a delicious red fruit on the inside. I consist of 92% water, and I originate from Africa.

Pineapple

  • Hi, my name is pineapple. I am a big fruit, about the size of your head! I have spiky leaves poking out of my top, and I have a rough spiky outer skin. My inside is tart, yellow and sweet. I am like no other fruit!
