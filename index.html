All About Fruits!

Apple

  • Hi, my name is Apple. I am a baseball sized, shiny red fruit. I have a crispy skin and juicy body, with a sweet taste. I come in different colors and sizes. I can be green, yellow, red or pink!

Banana

  • Hi, my name is Banana. I have a floppy yellow peel, with a sweet and creamy white fruit on the inside. I have a funky curved shape, with a hard stem that I am peeled from.

Pomegranate

  • Hi, my name is Pomegranate. I am a very unique fruit. I consist of a thick, hard and deep red shell. I have a colony of seeds inside me, with sweet and tart red fruit surrounding it. I am very delicious!

Strawberry

  • Hi, my name is Strawberry! I am one of the most famous fruits. I am a large and plump, cone shaped fruit that is red and shiny. I have seeds speckled all over my outer layer, and a green stem on my bottom. I am very sweet!

Blueberry

  • Hi, my name is Blueberry! Despite the name, I am really dark purple. I have a thin purple skin that pops when I am eaten. I have a juicy soft inside, with small seeds. I can be tart, or sweet!

Watermelon

  • Hi, my name is watermelon. I am a big red fruit, with a sweet and juicy taste. I have a hard, bitter outer rind, with a delicious red fruit on the inside. I consist of 92% water, and I originate from Africa.
